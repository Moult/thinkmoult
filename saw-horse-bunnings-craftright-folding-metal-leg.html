Saw horse Bunnings Craftright folding metal leg free 3D model

Back to Radiance models

This is a 3D model of a foldable saw horse by Craftright. It is sold by Bunnings in Australia. The saw horse has two orange folding metal legs, which have metal stops bolted to a timber plank with wingnuts, and rubber feet.

Materials

The colours have been calibrated with Macbethcal but the specularity and roughness values are guessed.

Usage

This Radiance model follows the Radiance Filesystem Hierarchy Standard. As such, please run make lib to build the Radiance mesh file. Once done, you can use this in your scene by:

!xform lib/saw-horse-bunnings-craftright-folding-metal-leg/obj/model.rad
