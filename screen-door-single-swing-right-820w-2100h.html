Screen door single swing right 820w 2100h free 3D model

Back to Radiance models

This is an off-white metal screen door. It has a fine mesh or flyscreen in the centre of its metal frame. It has a thin 20mm metal right and left jamb, and an 80mm metal top jamb.

No hardware is modeled, although in reality it has a handle. No hinges are modeled.

Materials

The colours have been calibrated with Macbethcal but the specularity and roughness values are guessed.

Usage

This Radiance model follows the Radiance Filesystem Hierarchy Standard. As such, please run make lib to build the Radiance mesh file. Once done, you can use this in your scene by:

!xform lib/screen-door-single-swing-right-820w-2100h/obj/model.rad
